Programs

2015-01-18

I've decided to start keeping a changelog and download link to the programs I'm developing on this webpage. Both for organization and consistency sake.

There are currently two programs that I've added to the website: BorderlandsBot and Osu Music Player

At the moment BorderlandsBot is under heavy development, but when it is done people who speedrun borderlands on Twitch will be able to add a bot to their channel (aka BorderlandsBot) and it will have general commands for the Borderlands games for questions that are asked fairly often. Once it is down, I plan for it to have custom commands, timers, and various other things. However, the first release will have preset commands that you can enable and disable.

Osu Music Player is - as you can hopefully tell - a music player. It's visuals are inspiried by a rhythm game that goes by the name of osu!. The objective with this was to create a music player that had a low profile on screen, but still look nice.
